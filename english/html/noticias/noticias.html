New contribution caps for the Contributory Regime

February 15

The Social Security Treasury established the new contribution caps for the contributory regime. These modifications are contained in Resolution 01-2024, issued by the institution, and came into effect as of February 1, 2024. According to the mentioned provision, the applicable national minimum wage for the calculation of contribution caps is RD$19,352.50.


This amount is used as a reference to determine the contributions corresponding to Work Risks Insurance, Family Health Insurance, and Old Age, Disability, and Survivor Insurance.
