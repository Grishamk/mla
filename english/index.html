Firm of certified public accountants that has been operating since 1983.

Offering audit and assurance, Tax Advisory, business and financial consulting services, as well as accounting and organizational development services with high international quality standards. Our main asset and key factor for success is our human capital, currently made up of more than 70 professionals.

MORE INFORMATION

Audit and Assurance

We conduct review and assurance processes in an independent, objective and timely manner aligned with international standards and best practices. We provide an accurate opinion on the general performance of the company, its processes and controls, thus creating value for the development and improvement of your organization.

MORE INFORMATION

Organizational Development

We offer specialized services focused on Organizational Development, which ensure that your strategy and internal resources operate efficiently, harmoniously and organized, allowing your company to adapt to the current and future needs in a planned and controlled manner.

MORE INFORMATION

Client Accounting Services (CAS)

Based on our specialization in accounting and tax matters in the different economic sectors of the country, we provide accounting outsourcing and tax compliance services that guarantees: the quality, reliability and timeliness of the financial information of your organization.

MORE INFORMATION

Tax Advisory

We provide specialized advisory and tax planning services, as well as defense and the reach of agreements with the regulatory authority, tax compliance and audits on the matter. Our services are based on risk management focused on tax efficiency under due compliance.
